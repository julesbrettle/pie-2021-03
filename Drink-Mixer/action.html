Our Machine In Action

Steps to operate the machine

  1. Plug the machine into a nearby power outlet. The LCD screen should light up indicating that the machine is on and ready to go.
  2. Provide an empty cup within the cup holder. While you can technically skip this step, it is not recommended to do so (there will be liquid everywhere).
  3. On the LCD screen, click the touch to begin button.
  4. There will be a prompt on whether an empty cup was placed. Select yes if there is one. If there is no empty cup, select no and get one.
  5. There will now be a list of drinks to select. Simply choose the one you want by pressing on the option.
  6. The drink will be poured and you can now take your cup! (Or repeat steps 3-5 to get a desired mixture).
